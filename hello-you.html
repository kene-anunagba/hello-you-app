The Name

Hello, you.

It's the first thing a warm voice says when it turns toward you. The greeting, not the pitch. Two small words that mean someone is glad you're here.

It never shouts, never sells. The comma is the whole idea. A small pause before the "you," the moment attention lands on the person across from you.

It says nothing about apps, chat, or matching. It just sounds like being welcomed.
